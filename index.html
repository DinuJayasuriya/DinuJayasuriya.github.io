Who I Am

Connecting Ecosystems
to Genomes

I'm a PhD candidate at Clemson University studying how microbial communities respond to environmental change ‚Äî from the open ocean and coastal estuaries to freshwater systems and wildfire-scorched soils.

What sets my research apart is the full-pipeline approach. I design and execute fieldwork, perform all wet lab processing, and carry out the bioinformatics analysis myself. Every decision ‚Äî from sample preservation to assembly parameters ‚Äî is made with the full picture in mind.

This blog documents that journey in real time, post by post, protocol by protocol.
